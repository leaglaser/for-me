Hello! I'm Lea

I am a passionate journalism and cinema/media studies student at the University of Maryland, College Park.


Currently, I am a sports writer for UMD's student-ran newspaper, The Diamondback. I cover the University of Maryland's gymnastics team, writing gamer stories per gymnastics meet along with two-day and midweek stories about the team. I also communicate with my editors and fellow sports writers to provide coverage of UMD sports via news. In the past, I covered women's soccer but love to explore all styles and mediums of journalism.


Aside from print journalism, I am passionated about cameras, videography, and film. I previously worked as a camera operator and lighting producer of a weekly newscast, This Week in Ocean City. I enjoy creating news packages with DSLR and broadcast cameras and have developed editing skills in Premiere Pro. I hope to continue my aspirations for writing and filmmaking.


I love learning new languages and watching movies in my free time! I am based in the Baltimore/Washington, D.C. area.
