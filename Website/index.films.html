Short Films



Below are short films (all under 10 minutes) that I wanted to explore.
I have always wanted to make films so tapping into a creative side of video was a way to express myself.




The Power of a Friendship | December 19, 2023 | 5:45
  • This project started December 31, 2022. I was determined to randomly record my friends being themselves through 2023, with hopes of eventually putting it together in a compilation, something we can look back on in a few years and reminisce. In the end, I took over 70 videos of my friends and I and put it together with music. I had recorded over 4 hours of footage and edited the project in Adobe Premiere Pro.




a man of a thousand barks | February 18, 2024 | 8:12
  • In February 2024, I said goodbye to my childhood dog. Through this, I wanted to remember him by putting together clips of him from years of capturing. In the end, I had over 50 videos that I sorted through to make the most of the project. I am really happy with the outcome of the project, it is something my family and I can look back on and remember for years to come. The project was edited in Adobe Premiere Pro.
